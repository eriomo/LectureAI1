ICAP Framework

The science behind deeper learning

Based on Chi & Wylie (2014). Each mode of engagement produces progressively deeper learning outcomes.

PASSIVE
Receiving
Listening, reading, watching. The foundation — building the knowledge base.
ACTIVE
Manipulating
Highlighting, repeating, annotating. Engaging with the material physically or mentally.
CONSTRUCTIVE
Generating
Explaining, creating, hypothesizing. Producing outputs beyond the material.
INTERACTIVE
Dialoguing
Debating, teaching, co-creating. The highest level — learning through dialogue.
